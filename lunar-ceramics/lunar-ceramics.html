About

Lunar Ceramics is where pottery meets the tranquility of a midnight garden. Experience handcrafted stoneware inspired by lunar phases and the raw beauty of the earth.

Beyond the wheel, we invite you to savor our artisanal coffee, brewed to perfection as you watch art come to life. Our space is your home for mindful creation and rest.

Learn Pottery

Unleash your inner artist in our guided workshops. From wheel-throwing basics to advanced glazing techniques, our expert instructors will help you master the craft at your own pace.

Artisanal Coffee

Lunar Ceramics is not only about clay and kilns. We proudly serve our own specially roasted artisanal coffee, crafted to awaken your senses.

Whether you need a creative boost during a pottery session or just want to relax in our peaceful studio environment, our coffee is here to complete your experience.

FAQ

Do I need experience?

Absolutely not! Our beginner workshops are designed for those who have never touched clay before.

Can I pick up my work?

Yes, your creations need time to dry and undergo two separate firings in our kiln. This process usually takes 2-3 weeks. We will notify you via SMS when your piece is glazed and ready for its new home.
